Uniform

  • Home
  • School Uniform
The School endeavours to inculcate habits of cleanliness and hygiene. Parents are therefore, requested to see that their children are always smartly dressed, not only to school but also, outside school and at public functions.

General Instructions

  • Uniform should be tailor made to fit the child decently as per his / her size.
  • School uniform socks should be ankle length and white in colour. There should be no logo / motif / emblem on it.
  • Students to wear ONLY black Sparx shoe with Velcro straps.
  • Nails should be clipped short and kept clean at all times.
  • School uniforms should be clean, neat and properly ironed each day.

Girls Uniform

  • Pinafore : The girls uniform should be a single pinafore made of dark blue spun material.
  • Belt : A dark blue belt of same material as the pinafore should be worn around the waist, the length of which should not exceed the length of the uniform.
  • Hair : Girls should have their hair neatly tied in two plaits with a middle parting. Each plait should be tied with a black satin ribbon. A black hair band should be used to keep all the hair neat and from falling on to the face.
  • Winter Wear : Girls to wear a plain maroon Cardigan. They must have the school’s name printed behind it.

Boys Uniform

  • Shirt : White T-shirt with school logo. The pocket must be on the left side of the T-shirt. A white vest must be worn under the T-shirt.
  • Shorts : The shorts should be made of dark blue terry cot material. There should be one pleat on each side of the shorts.
  • Hair : Boys must cut their hair short - not more than ½ an inch on the back and sides of the head, and one inch on the top and crown. Fancy haircuts like Mushroom cut, Quiff hair style, Spiky cuts, etc. are not acceptable. No other hair styles are acceptable. Sikh students may only use dark blue turbans.
  • Winter Wear : Boys to wear a plain maroon pullover during winters with a V-neck and long sleeves.They must have the school’s name printed behind it.

P.T. Uniform

    • The P.T. T-shirt should be of the house colour. School name to be printed behind the t-shirt.
    • The track pant has to be dark navy blue in colour in spun material.

Karate Dress

  • Girls & Boys : Karate dress must be tailor made to fit the child decently as per his / her size. It should be clean, neat and properly ironed each day. It should have logo as specified by the Karate instructor.
  • T-Shirt : The Uniform white T-shirt with school logo must be worn inside the karate dress.
