Beyond the Classroom

Co-Curricular Activities

Music

Music time is a popular and much loved activity in School, especially in the Nursery Section — class singing is fun time. In addition to class singing, we have a choir group from Std. I to IV which performs at various inter school choir festivals, competitions and other school functions.

Band

The School has its own percussion band. Students of Std. II to Std. IV are encouraged to learn musical instruments and form part of the band.

Karate

The well trained staff of 'All India Gojukai Karate – Do' founded by Mr. Vispy Kapadia conducts karate classes for students of Std. I to Std. IV during school hours.

Robotics

Robotics in schools provides hands-on learning by engaging students through a project-based approach. It fosters critical thinking, creativity, problem-solving, and teamwork skills, preparing students for future technological needs.

Excursions / Visits

The School organizes field trips / educational trips for the students to enhance experiential learning.

Bai Najamai Noshirwan Dastur Primary School is a recognized co-educational Primary School founded on 21st September 1953.
