Assessment & Advancement

Examination & Promotion

The Evaluation Pattern has been divided into Formative and Summative and the division of marks will be as follows. Formative evaluation will include Observations, Projects, Practicals, Oral Skills, Class Work, Home Work etc. Summative evaluation will comprise a Written Test which will be conducted twice a year.

Division of Marks

Std. I & II Std. III & IV
Formative 70 60
Summative 30 40

Promotion

The School follows promotion policy as per guidelines from Department of Education (Primary Section).

Bai Najamai Noshirwan Dastur Primary School is a recognized co-educational Primary School founded on 21st September 1953.
