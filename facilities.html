What We Offer

Facilities

Computer Lab

The School has a spacious and well-equipped state of the art computer labs to initiate the tiny tots to learn computer skills. Children of Std II & IV are also encouraged to join the computer club to further upgrade their computer knowledge.

Mazda Hall

The School's MAZDA HALL with the capacity of 350 seats, a well-equipped stage and acoustics provides an excellent facility to organise class assemblies, concerts, seminars, workshops and other cultural events.

Play Ground

Adequate open spaces and playgrounds are available within the School premises to organise sports and games like karate, basketball, football etc. & other outdoor activities.

Library

The School has a well-stocked library. Reference books, encyclopedias, story books, magazines etc. are available to inculcate a habit of reading and to generate curiosity in the young minds of our children.

Counselling and Guidance

In the present day competitive environment children are often subjected to stress. As the School aims to maintain a 'Child Centered' approach, a Guidance and Counselling Centre is available to help the children to cope with this stress and facilitate an all-round personality growth. The Centre also enables the children to identify their strengths and weaknesses. Through the process of counselling and guidance, most of the students do well in a classroom setting, while some need a more personalized and structured programme to enhance their potential. The Counsellor is available to the parents on all working days during the school hours.

Robotics Lab

The School has a spacious and well-equipped state of the art Robotics lab for hands-on learning, where students use robotics kits, computers, and software to build, program, and control robots. Children of Std III & IV are also encouraged to join the Robotics club to further upgrade their skill.

Insurance

Sardar Dastur Schools Trust provides 'Student, Staff Safety Insurance Policy' from United India Insurance Company Ltd., under which every child / ward and staff member is insured against accidents.

Bai Najamai Noshirwan Dastur Primary School is a recognized co-educational Primary School founded on 21st September 1953.
