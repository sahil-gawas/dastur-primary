School Discipline

General Rules

The School Gate will close 5 minutes before the bell time. Students arriving late will not be allowed to enter the School.

On School days, Open Parents Day and functions, students should wear the School uniform. Those who come to School in a shabby and improper uniform will be marked absent. One week is allowed for newly admitted students in June to acquire the uniform.

No Student can go back home before the School ends without the written permission of the Principal.

The School Calendar must be brought to School daily and should be maintained carefully. Pupils who come to School without Calendar will be marked absent.

Strict regularity, implicit obedience, politeness and courtesy in speech and conduct, cleanliness of books, dress and person are rigidly insisted upon.

CHILDREN ARE NOT ALLOWED TO APPLY NAIL POLISH, MEHENDI OR TATTOOS OR WEAR ANY JEWELLERY AROUND THE NECK OR PAYALS, BANGLES, ETC., WHEN ATTENDING SCHOOL.

Bai Najamai Noshirwan Dastur Primary School is a recognized co-educational Primary School founded on 21st September 1953.
